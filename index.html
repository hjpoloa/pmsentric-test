/ About PMSentric

Practitioners.

PMSentric is built by project controls professionals who use these tools on real projects every day.

The Mission

Project management professionals deserve tools that match how they actually work — opening XER files on a client's laptop, reviewing schedules in the field, producing reports under deadline. Most software in this space is built around enterprise sales cycles, not real scheduling workflows.

PMsentric exists to fill that gap: fast, browser-native tools that require zero installation and respect your data privacy. No cloud uploads. No IT approvals. No waiting.

Core Principles

🔒
Privacy by Design

⚡

💡
Real Problems Only
Every feature comes from a real scheduling need, not a roadmap meeting or a feature request tracker.

PMsentric is built on a privacy-first principle: your project data never leaves your machine. Schedule PrimeReader processes XER files entirely in your browser. We never see, store, or transmit your schedule content.

2. Information We Collect

Account & purchase data. When you buy a license, our payment provider (Paddle) processes your name, email, billing address, and payment method. We store only the minimum needed to fulfill your order: email address and license key.

Basic usage analytics. We may collect anonymous, aggregated data about how our website is used (page views, browser type, country) to improve our products. This data is not linked to your identity.

Support communications. If you email us or use our contact form, we retain your message to respond and for quality assurance.

  • Your project activities, WBS structure, or any data loaded into the tool.

7. Your Rights
